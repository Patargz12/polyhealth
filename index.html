WHAT IS POLYHEALTH?


PolyHealth strives to provide you quality healthcare services and is committed to the highest quality standards of patient care. Our competent staff and nurses and dedicated doctors are here to give you the care that you deserve. Feel free to reach out if you need any health assistance as long as it is under the scope of our expertise.

GOALS / OBJECTIVES


PolyHealth’s aim is to provide comprehensive and consistent healthcare for individuals and families. PolyHealth centers its efforts to emphasize disease prevention and predictive medicine based on the patient in view of the family and the community. PolyHealth applies a new perspective to the ways of the clinic as its team of reliable doctors apply their knowledge and skills to residents of the community that would otherwise have no access to high quality medical and dental care.

VISION

To be a leading healthcare provider known for its quality medical and dental services committed to serve the community of Malabon and local corporate and industrial healthcare requirements.



MISSION

At Polyhealth, we believe that our obligations and responsibilities do not simply lie in achieving wellness for certain individuals only.
Polyhealth aims to serve communities in remote or far-flung locations that do not have access to qualified healthcare practitioners or fully equipped medical facilities.
We believe that the best way to serve a community is to serve it equally well without bias or partiality where patients from all walks of life will be treated with equal degrees of respect, dignity, attention, and compassion.
