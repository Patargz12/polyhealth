OUR STORY

Dr. Francus Galio, Dr. Manya & Dr. Rodolfo all worked for significant dental corporations after graduating from dental school, gaining years of valuable expertise. However, they felt that they could give more to patients in the Philippines due to the impersonal character of big business dentistry. The doctors wanted to open a practice where they could spend time getting to know their patients and understanding their requirements, as well as build a welcoming environment for both patients and staff. We go above and above at PolyHealth to provide high-quality, customized treatment, and we always make time for each patient.
